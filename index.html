- This is who we are -

OWWR, Old Westbury Web Radio is the official radio station of SUNY Old Westbury, on the air streaming audio and video 24/7. OWWR provides student, alumni, faculty, staff, and community volunteer programming that broadcasts online, and via any wireless device or tablet.

Primary Departments • Education• Music• Production • Programming• Public Affairs• Public Relations • News• Sports• Maintenance

OWWR provides a variety of promotional opportunities for performers and venues, including in-house promos (:30 or :60), social networking updates, and ‘Live Reads’.

In conjunction with the mission of our college, OWWR is an active member in the community. Our student staff members implement their critical thinking skills with live news, sports, and educational webcasts, as seen on-line.

OWWR also offers tax deductible underwriting opportunities. Contact us for more information.
